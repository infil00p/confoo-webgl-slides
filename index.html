WebVR Boilerplate

  • Created in 2015 by Boris Smus
  • Good starting point for WebVR across multiple devices

Advantages of using just Boilerplate

  • You get more control over the 3D scene, objects, etc
  • If you know three.js, you know how to use the Boilerplate
  • three_js can import STL files

A-Frame
